IDENTIFY AS A RUSTBUILDER

The Rust Belt is comprised of seven middle Atlantic states with a common bond as the creators and producers of the industrial revolution who have since been challenged to repeat such value creation.

RustBuilt provides a common united voice for these communities to reclaim the innovation and leadership mantle on which they used to stand.

Lend your support by downloading and using the RustBuilt logos and referencing the RustBuilt URLs on your products, solutions and innovations. Help us show the world that this community is strong and help us (literally) count the number of innovators in the region.

ABOUT RUSTBUILT

RustBuilt.org is a not-for-profit initiative with the sole purpose of shedding a light on the collective and valuable innovation and entrepreneurialism of the Rust Belt.

It provides a common unified voice for these communities to reclaim the innovation and leadership mantle on which they used to stand.
