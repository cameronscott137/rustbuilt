ABOUT RUSTBUILT

RustBuilt.org is a not-for-profit initiative with the sole purpose of shedding a light on the collective and valuable innovation and entrepreneurialism of the Rust Belt.

It provides a common unified voice for these communities to reclaim the innovation and leadership mantle on which they used to stand.
